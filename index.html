Completion Requirements
🎵 Clicks Must Be Audible
▼
Your completion video and raw footage must have all clicks fully audible throughout the entire completion. Clickbots are not acceptable and are banned entirely.
🎬 Video Upload Required
▼
Upload your completion to: YouTube, Vimeo, Twitch, BiliBili, Medal, or Outplayed. Completion videos may not be deleted after acceptance.
🛡️ Cheat Indicators
▼
If using mods with an endscreen cheat indicator, all indicators must be clearly visible and show no cheats were used. Applies to records after July 2nd, 2023.
📊 Level Stats on Endscreen
▼
Your record must show stats on the endscreen (attempts, orbs, jumps, time). If you've beaten the level before, note this in Additional Notes.
🎞️ Raw Footage (Top Levels)
▼
Top 400 levels require raw footage with an isolated click audio track. Upload via Google Drive or similar. Raw footage may be deleted after review.
⚙️ No Disallowed Mods
▼
No bots, hacked completions, Physics Bypass, or red/blue cheat indicators. If using a mod menu, the Cheat Indicator on Endscreen must be enabled.
⏭️ No Significant Skips
▼
No skips that make sections significantly easier. Contact a moderator if unsure about a specific skip.
👥 Two Player Rules
▼
All completions must be by a single player unless the level is a 2P level. Solo 2P completions need a visible webcam showing both hands.
